Campus Life

Euroschool North Campus

Every child discovers their potential from a universe of possibilities. Our endeavour at EuroSchool is to encourage children to realise latent talent, ascertain areas of interest, hone individual skills, thereby uncovering varied facets of their personality. Through this journey of self-discovery, they are guided to develop their abilities, transforming themselves into responsible citizens of tomorrow.One of the best schools in Bangalore, EuroSchool North Campus has a well-designed layout and a specially crafted spacious environment offering a magnificent ambience to nurture young minds. The school provides curriculum in accordance with the CBSE Board. EuroSchool North Campus has a gamut of exploration areas that include specially designed modern laboratories, reading rooms and libraries, state of the art computer labs, multi-purpose playgrounds, professional sports coaches, and ‘smart-class’ technology-enabled classrooms

Balanced Learning

EuroSchool subscribes to Balanced Schooling, which is the cornerstone of our Pedagogy, to provide a holistic development of the child in a conducive learning environment. This approach to learning aims at creating confident and responsible individuals through a combination of Academics and Co-Curricular activities. LRPA forms the crux of our pedagogy at EuroSchool; where students Learn – Reinforce – Practice – Apply knowledge, concepts and skills. This pedagogical model enables the student to become an independent learner, aided by technology and facilitated by the teacher. In this journey of self-discovery, children are guided by our expert educators through engagement programmes which build 21st-century skills like lateral and critical thinking, creativity and reasoning.

Digital Learing

At EuroSchool, our commitment to creating lifelong learners has powered us to leverage technology to bring 'Schooling Home' for your child. Our virtual schooling program runs on our proprietary Digital Learning Ecosystem, ARGUS. This application, in combination with Live Classroom sessions, has enabled our expert educators to seamlessly blend Digitally enhanced Textbooks, recorded lectures, home assignments & Student Collaboration with digital assessments. By ensuring active tracking of learning milestones through Argus and modules for conducting periodic online assessments, we are coming closer to our objective of creating a personalised learning experience which will create Engaged, Self-motivated and Independent Learners
